Learn languages by speaking with Soliloquy!

The ability to speak in a foreign language is the key goal of learning. Practice speaking with Soliloquy and learn faster.

Learn in a meaningful way

With Soliloquy, you can practice speaking a new language and build your own personalized phrasebook.

Our flashcard system and spaced repetition technique help you remember new words and phrases more effectively, so you can make progress faster.
