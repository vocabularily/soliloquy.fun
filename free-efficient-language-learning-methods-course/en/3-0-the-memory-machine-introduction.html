Module 3: The Memory Machine - Vocabulary & Grammar a Better Way

Module 3: Introduction

Congratulations on successfully completing the Module 2 checkpoint! You've proven that you have the essential skill of an independent learner: the ability to find your own high-quality input. Your brain is now primed with a steady stream of new language.

But this leads to a new, crucial question: How do you make sure you remember what you're learning?

Welcome to the next stage of our journey.

Your Goal for the Next 2 Weeks: To build a reliable system for moving new words and grammar from your short-term recognition into your long-term, active memory, all without the pain of rote memorization.

Let's begin with the single most powerful tool in a language learner's arsenal.
